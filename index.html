About Me

I have worked in marketing. In this field, I was shown a glimpse into the world of programming. From there my love of it grew. JavaScript is a particularly enjoyable language because of its complexity. The most exciting part of programming is the creative problem solving. I am also a fast learner, which transfers well into the always changing world of programming.

I received my Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Utah, where I developed a deep love of art. This appreciation transfers to web development with web aesthetics and flow. I am a full stack web developer who loves programming and also enjoys creating short Lego stop motion videos.
